SUPPLIES 
==========
- 6 x cotton fabric circles. Quilting cotton works the best. Cut 4" (10cm) diameter for the larger flower or 3" (7.5cm) diameter circles for the smaller flowers.  
- hot glue gun
- buttons or beads to decorate
- 1" (2.5cm) diameter circle of felt or fabric to glue to the back
- optional - lined alligator clip to glue to the back.
